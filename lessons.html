About me

Iâ€™m Illia Netkach, a professional and experienced violin teacher with a Junior Bachelor's Degree from Kharkiv State College of Music (Ukraine) and a Licentiate Diploma in Violin Teaching from the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (UK).
I picked up the violin at the age of 7 and have been following my passion ever since. My teaching journey in music began long before graduation, teaching my younger cousins and discovering my love for sharing knowledge. Though I dreamed of a performer career, I always felt the irresistible call to teach and mentor.
In 2013 I moved to the Middle East, and my teaching experience here has been incredibly enriching - guiding students of diverse ages, nationalities, religions, and cultures. Many of my students have excelled in exams, competitions, and have simply become talented players.
During the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, I transitioned to online teaching and was amazed by its effectiveness when approached the right way. In my lessons, I fully leverage the power of available technology, which includes professional software, a good camera, and decent microphones to deliver the best quality to the student.
They say an expert is someone who has made all the possible mistakes in their field, and that's me. I've walked this path, and now you can too. Whether you want your child to learn this beautiful instrument or you have always dreamed of playing the violin yourself, now is the perfect time to start. Get in touch, and let me be your guide on this wonderful musical journey.
